## 📌 Project Overview
This project transforms raw e-commerce transaction data into an interactive, user-friendly dashboard to uncover purchasing trends and actionable business insights. The goal was to provide a clear, visual "story" behind the numbers, allowing stakeholders to easily evaluate regional performance, segment behavior, and category profitability.

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings
* **Revenue Drivers:** Technology is the highest-performing category, generating $0.83M in sales, followed closely by Furniture ($0.73M) and Office Supplies ($0.71M). 
* **Seasonal Surges:** Sales volume increases significantly during Q4, with the most dramatic revenue spikes occurring in November. 
* **Customer & Regional Dominance:** The West region contributes the highest overall revenue, while the "Consumer" segment accounts for the largest share of the 793 total customers.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
Based on the dashboard insights, I developed the following strategic recommendations:
* **Capitalize on Strengths:** Focus marketing campaigns and ad spend on high-performing Technology products.
* **Optimize Inventory:** Prepare additional inventory and optimize supply chain logistics ahead of the high-demand Q4 months (specifically September through November).
* **Target Growth Opportunities:** Increase promotional activities in lower-performing regions (like the South) and improve targeting strategies to capture more of the Corporate and Home Office segments.

## 🛠️ Skills & Tools Demonstrated
* **Technical Execution:** Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Interactive Filtering, Map Visualizations).
* **Data Storytelling & Business Acumen:** Designed an intuitive interface focused on end-user experience, moving beyond raw metrics to provide actionable business recommendations.
* **Adaptability & Problem Solving:** Approached an unstructured e-commerce dataset, organized 5K total orders, and designed a cohesive final product.
